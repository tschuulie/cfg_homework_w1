Welcome to my HTML website about Icelandic Sheepdogs

The Icelandic Sheepdog is a breed of dog of spitz type originating from the dogs brought to Iceland by the Vikings. It is of similar type to the Norwegian Buhund, the Shetland Sheepdog, and the Welsh Corgi. They are commonly used to herd sheep in the Icelandic countryside. Source: Wikipedia

Three facts about Icelandic Sheepdogs

  1. They originate from the dogs brought to Iceland by the Vikings.
  2. Icelandic Sheepdogs are known to be energetic and friendly.
  3. The breed has been close to extinction multiple times.
